Recipe for Pot Roast

Description

This pot roast features very juicy roast with delectable potatoes and carrots. It will knock your socks off.

Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • White Onion
  • Garlic
  • Seasoning
  • Broth

Directions

  1. Start by browning the chuck roast in a large pan. I usually do this in the instant pot.
  2. Saute the onion and garlic.
  3. Add in the meat, carrots, and potatoes. Add broth and cook for an hour or so.
